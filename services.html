Comprehensive Cargo Solutions

We offer a wide range of marine cargo and logistics services to meet your shipping needs

Domestic Shipping

Reliable and efficient cargo delivery services within the United Arab Emirates. We provide fast and secure transportation across all emirates including Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, Fujairah, Ras Al Khaimah, and Umm Al Quwain.

Our Domestic Shipping Includes:

  • Door-to-door delivery within UAE
  • Same-day and next-day delivery options
  • LCL (Less than Container Load) services
  • Full truckload and partial shipments
  • Real-time tracking updates
  • Insurance coverage available

International Shipping

Global cargo shipping services to Pakistan, India, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, and other worldwide destinations. Our extensive network ensures your cargo reaches any destination safely and on time.

International Destinations:

  • Pakistan (Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad)
  • India (Mumbai, Delhi, Chennai)
  • Bangladesh (Dhaka, Chittagong)
  • Sri Lanka (Colombo)
  • Worldwide container shipping
  • FCL and LCL options

Freight Forwarding

Complete end-to-end logistics solutions for your business. Our experienced freight forwarding team handles every aspect of your shipment from pickup to final delivery.

Freight Forwarding Services:

  • Sea freight (FCL/LCL)
  • Air freight for urgent shipments
  • Multi-modal transport
  • Cargo insurance
  • Import/Export documentation
  • Consolidation services

Express Cargo

Time-critical shipments handled with priority. When you need your cargo delivered fast, our express services ensure fastest possible delivery without compromising on safety.

Express Services Include:

  • Priority handling and processing
  • Express sea freight (3-5 days to Pakistan)
  • Door-to-door express delivery
  • Overnight shipping options
  • Dedicated customer support
  • Time-definite deliveries

Warehousing & Logistics

Secure storage facilities in Sharjah for your cargo. Our warehousing services provide safe storage and efficient inventory management for businesses of all sizes.

Warehousing Solutions:

  • Secure storage facilities
  • Short-term and long-term storage
  • Inventory management
  • Pick and pack services
  • Distribution and fulfillment
  • Climate-controlled options

Customs Clearance

Professional customs brokerage and clearance services in UAE. We handle all documentation and customs procedures to ensure smooth and hassle-free import/export operations.

Customs Services Include:

  • Import customs clearance (UAE)
  • Export customs clearance
  • Documentation services
  • Duty and tax calculations
  • Regulatory compliance
  • Cargo inspection coordination
