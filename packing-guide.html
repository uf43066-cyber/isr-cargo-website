Prohibited & Restricted Items

The following items cannot be shipped due to legal and safety regulations.

Absolute Prohibited

  • Weapons & Ammunition
  • Explosives & Fireworks
  • Drugs & Narcotics
  • Hazardous Chemicals
  • Live Animals
  • Counterfeit Goods

Restricted Items

  • Lithium Batteries (special packaging)
  • Alcohol (permit required)
  • Pharmaceuticals (prescription needed)
  • Precious Metals (declare value)
  • Food Items (sealed, shelf-stable)
  • Plants (phytosanitary cert)

Special Handling Required

  • Electronics (bubble wrap)
  • Glass Items (extra padding)
  • Art & Antiques (custom crate)
  • Musical Instruments (hard case)
  • Medical Equipment (declaration)
  • Perishables (ice pack needed)

Expert Packing Tips

Follow these tips to ensure your items arrive safely.

Use Quality Materials

Use sturdy boxes, bubble wrap, packing peanuts, and quality tape for maximum protection.

Fill All Gaps

Fill empty spaces with packing material to prevent items from shifting during transit.

Weight Distribution

Place heavier items at the bottom and lighter items on top to prevent damage.

Label Clearly

Mark boxes with "Fragile" or "This Side Up" and list contents on the outside.

Photo Documentation

Take photos of valuable items before packing for insurance purposes.

Don't Overpack

Leave some cushioning space. Boxes should close easily without force.
