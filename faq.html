Common Questions About Our Services

Find answers to frequently asked questions about marine cargo shipping

What documents do I need for shipping?

Required documents vary based on shipment type, but typically include:

  • Copy of passport/visa (sender and recipient)
  • Commercial invoice
  • Packing list
  • Trade license (for commercial shipments)

Our team will provide specific document requirements based on your shipment.

How long does shipping to Pakistan take?

Shipping times to Pakistan vary by service type:

  • Standard Sea Freight: 10-14 days
  • Express Sea Freight: 5-7 days
  • Air Freight: 2-3 days

Note: Additional time may be required for customs clearance at destination.

What is the minimum weight for shipping?

We accept shipments of all sizes. For small parcels (under 20kg), our express services are ideal. For larger shipments, we offer both LCL (Less than Container Load) and FCL (Full Container Load) options. Contact us for a custom quote regardless of shipment size.

How is shipping cost calculated?

Shipping costs are calculated based on:

  • Weight and dimensions (volumetric weight)
  • Origin and destination
  • Service type (standard/express)
  • Type of cargo (general/special handling)
  • Insurance coverage (if selected)

Use our Get a Quote form for an accurate estimate.

Can I send personal belongings to Pakistan?

Yes, we handle personal shipments regularly. You'll need to provide:

  • Copy of sender's passport
  • Copy of recipient's CNIC (Pakistan ID)
  • Detailed packing list of household items

Our team will guide you through the process for personal shipments.

What items are prohibited for shipping?

Prohibited items typically include:

  • Explosives and flammable materials
  • Weapons and ammunition
  • Drugs and narcotics
  • Counterfeit goods
  • Perishable food items
  • Live animals

Contact us for specific questions about restricted items.

Do you provide insurance for shipments?

Yes, we offer cargo insurance options to protect your shipments. Insurance cost is typically 1-2% of the declared cargo value. We recommend insurance for valuable or fragile items. Our team can explain coverage options when you request a quote.

What happens if my shipment is damaged?

In the rare event of damage:

  1. Document the damage with photos immediately upon delivery
  2. Contact our customer support within 48 hours
  3. File a claim with supporting documentation

If you have insurance, claims are processed within 7-14 business days. For uninsured shipments, we'll investigate and provide compensation based on our terms.

Do you handle customs clearance?

Yes, we provide complete customs clearance services in UAE:

  • Import customs clearance (UAE)
  • Export customs clearance
  • Documentation preparation
  • Duty and tax calculations
  • Regulatory compliance

Our experienced team ensures smooth customs processing.
